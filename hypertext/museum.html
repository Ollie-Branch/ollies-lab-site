This is a place for abandoned projects, gone but not forgotten. I only put projects in here that are for sure cancelled because whatever purpose I could have gotten from them has long since been fulfilled.

Sometimes, it is important to realize that a project no longer serves you or your goals as things change, or that whatever you needed a project for has been fulfilled.

It's not impossible for a piece in the museum to be picked back up, but it's unlikely. These are the projects that are abandoned for good reason.
